Multiple Sclerosis elastic-net GWAS paper - extra data


This page acts as a source of access to extra information supporting our publication using an elastic-net approach to feature selection in a GWAS manner for multiple sclerosis. If you are looking for the repository source for this page you can find it here. Below is the citation for the manuscript.

Citation: Burnard et al. 2020. Beyond conventional GWAS: Interaction between NK receptor loci and the HLA in Multiple Sclerosis revealed by targeted penalised regression models. (CURRENTLY UNPUBLISHED)
